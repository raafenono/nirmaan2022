QUIZZING CAMEOS

About event

Warm welcome, To all the students out there, the Nirmaan 2020 by civil department, College of Engineering, OSMANIA UNIVERSITY brings to you...the quizzing cameos.

Quizzing cameos is a technical event which involves questions related to structures, hydrology, environmental engineering, construction management and geomatics.

The event is simple but significant and tests your academic abilities.

There will different rounds to analyze your skills, memory and knowledge.

Outline will be given one week prior to the event.

Rules

   - The contestents shall be in pairs and there will a registration fee of 150/- for each pair.
   - The winner of the contest will be awarded with cash prize
   - All the participants will be given the participation certificates
   - The contest will be held in the civil department
